Who I Am

About Me

I am a Portfolio Analyst in the Digital Lending Unit at Stanbic Bank Uganda, where I joined in August 2025. Since joining, I have built and deployed the unit's core reporting and analytics infrastructure — eliminating manual processes and giving management real-time visibility into portfolio health, recoveries, and lending performance.

My responsibilities extend to the Recoveries and Rehabilitation Unit, where I develop tools and frameworks that support recovery tracking and compliance oversight. I also led the NSSF–Stanbic strategic partnership initiative from initial presentation through to proposal submission.

I work across the full analytics stack: SQL for data extraction from Finacle, Power BI for dashboards and management reporting, R for statistical modelling and automation, and Excel for structured analysis.

I am currently pursuing a Master of Statistics at Makerere University, focusing on statistical inference, statistical computing, and machine learning — with the goal of building more advanced credit risk models for digital lending.
